ACurriculum Vitae (CV) is a document used for job applications that provides a comprehensive summary of an individual's educational background, work experience, skills, and achievements. Unlike a shorter resume, a CV is typically more detailed and is used to apply for academic, research, or professional positions that require in-depth information about the applicant's qualifications.

Student Day Activity is an event organized by Politeknik Negeri Manado as an appreciation of Nusantara culture. This event has been held several times by Politeknik Negeri Manado. On May 17, 2024, the event also served as a farewell for students who participated in the student exchange program at Politeknik Negeri Manado. The event aims to introduce and celebrate the diversity of Indonesian culture through various activities such as cultural exhibitions, art performances, and traditional cuisine. Additionally, the farewell moment for the exchange students added an emotional touch to the event, providing an opportunity for them to reflect on the experiences and learning they gained while at Politeknik Negeri Manado. Through the Student Day Activity, Politeknik Negeri Manado hopes to strengthen the sense of unity and love for local culture among students, as well as foster closer relationships between local and international students.
